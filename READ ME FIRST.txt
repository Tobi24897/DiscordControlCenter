============================================================
  DISCORD CONTROL CENTER  —  Quick Start
============================================================

All your trading Discords, X feeds, and news in ONE clean
dashboard. Runs locally on your Windows PC. No install, free.


HOW TO START (3 steps):
----------------------------------------

  1) UNZIP this folder first (if it is still inside the ZIP):
     right-click the ZIP -> "Extract All".
     IMPORTANT: do not start it from inside the ZIP!

  2) Double-click:
       "Start Discord Control Center.vbs"
     -> Your browser opens with the dashboard.
     (If Windows warns: "More info" -> "Run anyway".
      That is normal, not a virus.)

  3) In SETTINGS at the top, log in to Discord once:
     drag the "Discord Login" button into your browser's
     bookmarks bar, then click it while on discord.com.
     (Don't see the bookmarks bar? Press Ctrl+Shift+B -
      it's the thin bar under the address bar.)
     Done - you're logged in.


QUIT:
----------------------------------------
  Just close the browser tab. The tool shuts itself down.
  The next double-click starts it fresh.


DESKTOP ICON (optional):
----------------------------------------
  Double-click "Create Desktop Shortcut.vbs"
  -> puts a start icon on your desktop.


NEED HELP / SOMETHING STUCK?
----------------------------------------
  The full guide with every step and troubleshooting is in
  the file  GUIDE.md  (open it with a text editor or read it
  on GitHub).


NOTE:
----------------------------------------
  The tool uses your personal Discord login to read the
  Discord API (like DiscordChatExporter). This violates
  Discord's Terms of Service - use at your own risk. It only
  reads slowly/gently; messages are always sent by YOU,
  never automatically.

  Your data stays local on your PC. Your login is sent only
  to Discord, nowhere else.
